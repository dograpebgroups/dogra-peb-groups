Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our construction, fabrication, and infrastructure solutions.

What are the key benefits of PEB construction? +

Pre-Engineered Buildings offer faster construction, cost efficiency, factory-controlled quality, design flexibility, and reduced material waste compared to conventional construction methods.

How long does it take to build a prefabricated house? +

Prefabricated houses are typically completed much faster than traditional construction, with factory manufacturing followed by quick on-site assembly, depending on design and customization.

What is the difference between MS and SS tanks? +

Mild Steel (MS) tanks are economical and suitable for general storage, while Stainless Steel (SS) tanks provide superior corrosion resistance and hygiene, making them ideal for food, pharmaceutical, and chemical use.

Are your playway and open gym equipment safe for public use? +

Yes. Our playway and open gym equipment are designed with safety, durability, and user comfort in mind, using child-safe materials, rounded edges, and robust, weather-resistant steel structures.
