Our Company

Established in 2011, Dogra PEB Groups has emerged as a leading provider of Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) solutions and construction services across India. We specialize in delivering innovative, cost-effective, and timely construction solutions for various industries.

Our expertise spans across PEB construction, prefabricated houses, industrial storage tanks, and recreational infrastructure including playground and gym equipment.

What We Do

PEB Construction: We design and construct Pre-Engineered Buildings using factory-made steel components that are assembled on-site, offering faster construction, cost savings, and design flexibility for warehouses, factories, schools, and commercial buildings.

Prefabricated Houses: We build modern, affordable factory-built homes that can be assembled on-site in 30-60 days, offering 10-20% cost savings compared to traditional construction methods.

MS/SS Tanks: We fabricate and install durable storage tanks for water, chemicals, fuel, and food products. Our MS tanks are cost-effective with protective coatings, while SS tanks offer superior corrosion resistance for hygienic applications.

Playway & Gym Equipment: We design and install safe, engaging playground equipment for physical development and durable, weather-resistant open gym equipment for public spaces.
